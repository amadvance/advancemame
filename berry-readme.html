AdvanceBERRY is a ready-to-use Raspberry Pi 3/4 image that transforms your Pi into a plug-and-play arcade gaming console for your TV.

Originally created as a Christmas gift for children (and their parents), it is designed for users with little to no computer experience.

It works out of the box, no keyboard required, even at first boot. Gamepads are automatically detected, including Bluetooth models.

It boots in just a few seconds and launches directly into a visually rich menu featuring animated previews of available games. Gameplay visuals are enhanced with scaling effects, like XBR, that are intelligently selected at runtime to ensure smooth performance without any slowdown.

You can get more info from the readme.txt and more internal details from tech.txt.
