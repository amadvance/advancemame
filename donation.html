If you consider that Advance Project is worth at least a little donation, and if you like to support an Open Source project, here is your opportunity to contribute by giving a financial aid to the author.

Building a project, even an open source project, is not cheap at all. Even though I work for free still there are bills to pay. Moreover, the time consumption is really a lot and, as you probably know, "time is money".

Please understand that in case you give money, I will not provide you with any sort of guarantee or promise.

Donation

The minimum donation is 20 EURO (1 EURO is approx 1.2 US$). You can donate more simply selecting a quantity greater than one in the order form. Any unit corresponds to 20 EURO.

Visa, Eurocard/MasterCard, American Express, Diners Club are accepted. The donation form is available in different languages:

English, German, Portuguese, Spanish, Italian and French.
